05 — Acquisition
Lead Generation Ecosystem

Seven major channels form a complete acquisition system designed to operate at scale with minimal marketing expenditure.

CH — 01
College Outreach
Final-year students are highly motivated and actively seeking opportunities. Target Physical Education, Sports Science, MBA, Social Work, and Leadership departments through HODs, Placement Officers, and Sports Directors.
Workflow: Identify → Proposal → Follow-up → Info Session → Collect Applications → Partnership

Expected Output: 100–500 leads per institution
CH — 02
University Partnerships
Priority engagement with State Universities, Private Universities, Sports Universities, and leadership-focused institutions for large-scale, long-term institutional partnerships.
Impact: Large-scale lead generation

Benefits: Long-term partnerships · Institutional credibility
CH — 03
LinkedIn Acquisition
The most precise acquisition channel. Targeted searches for Sports Coaches, Leadership Trainers, Career Coaches, Fitness Trainers, Youth Mentors, and PE Teachers.
Daily Target: 50–100 messages
Weekly Target: 350–700 messages

Expected: 5–15 quality conversations daily
CH — 04
WhatsApp Communities
Partner with admins in sports academies, coaching institutes, NSS groups, NCC groups, university communities, and professional trainer groups to distribute opportunity materials.
Potential Reach: 500–5,000 per community

Approach: Admin partnerships · Existing audiences
CH — 05
Telegram Communities
Join existing coaching, fitness, leadership, and government exam mentor communities. Create branded channels and share valuable content daily to build organic reach.
Strategy: Join communities → Create channels → Daily content

Focus: Coaching · Fitness · Leadership groups
CH — 06
Strategic Partnerships
Forge alliances with Sports Authority of India, State Sports councillors, Toastmasters, NSS, NCC, HR Associations, and Entrepreneurship Cells for structured, recurring lead pipelines.
Partners: SAI · State councillors · Toastmasters
NSS · NCC · HR Associations

Type: Sports · Leadership · Professional orgs
CH — 07
Referral Engine
The most scalable long-term acquisition method. Existing applicants, selected candidates, alumni, coaches, and mentors generate new leads through certificates, recognition, ambassador status, and recommendation letters.
Rewards: Certificates · Recognition · Ambassador status

Outcome: Self-sustaining growth
